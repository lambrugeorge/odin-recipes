Chicken Lemon

A refreshing and zesty recipe perfect for a family meal

Description

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons (juiced)
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Optional Ingredients

  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of honey (for sweetness)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, combine lemon juice, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, and oregano. Add chicken breasts and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  3. Arrange in a baking dish: Place marinated chicken breasts in a greased baking dish. Pour any remaining marinade over the chicken.
  4. Bake the chicken: Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
  5. Optional step: For a richer flavor, add chicken broth and honey before baking.
  6. Garnish and serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with your choice of side dishes, like rice or vegetables.
