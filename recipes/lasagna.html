Lasagna

Tasty and easy to prepare recipe for a family dinner

Description

Ingredients

Ragu

  • 600g minced meat mixture
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/2 yellow pepper
  • 250g peeled tomatoes (I used from Sun Food)
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 sprigs of green parsley
  • 3-4 sprigs of green dill

Bechamel

  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g flour
  • 600 ml milk
  • salt
  • pepper

Lasagna Sheets

  • 9 lasagna sheets (this was needed for my dish)
  • 150 g cheese
  • 1 teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Chop the onion, peel and grate the carrot, dice the bell pepper, and chop the tomatoes.
  2. Sauté the vegetables: Heat oil in a pan, add the onion, bell pepper, and carrot, and sauté for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the meat: Incorporate the minced meat and stir well. Add a cup of water and let it simmer for 10 minutes. Add the tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, then add the tomato paste and let it cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Prepare the bechamel sauce: In a thicker-bottomed pot, melt the butter, add the flour, mix, and gradually pour in the milk, stirring continuously until it thickens. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: In a greased baking dish, add a layer of béchamel sauce, 3 lasagna sheets, half of the meat mixture, béchamel sauce, and repeat the layers (lasagna sheets, meat sauce, lasagna sheets, and the remaining béchamel on top).
  6. Finish: Grate cheese and sprinkle it on top.
  7. Bake: Preheat the oven and bake the lasagna for 50-55 minutes until golden. Let it cool slightly before serving.
